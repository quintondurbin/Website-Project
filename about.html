Hickory Pit BBQ restaurant was est. 1978 by a Hardin County local. Ownership of the establishment was then sold to another local, Beth Crunk in 1997. The Hickory Pit BBQ restaurant is still thriving today in the Historic District of Savannah TN. They are open Monday-Saturday and have great BBQ, smoked daily on site, for an affordable price.
